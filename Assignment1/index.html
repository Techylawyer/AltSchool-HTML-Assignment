My AltSchool Africa Journey

Meet Me

Hi there! My name is Grace Oluwasegun, with AltSchool ID ALT/SOE/024/1794. I am a lawyer, having being called to the Nigerian bar in 2017, and I have over five years' experience working in the legal tech space.

Why AltSchool Africa?

In 2019, Tech found me after I accepted an offer to join a legal tech startup as an analyst. After a while, I became dissatisfied with my 9-5 and I decided to take a deep dive into the world of tech.

I started off with learning product management. After a 3-week bootcamp in product management, I knew there was more and I began seeking more knowledge. One of the product management tutors emphasized that technical knowledge is required to become an excellent product manager.

My desire to be excellent at anything I do fuelled my signing up on FreeCodeCamp to learn the basics of web development. However, after I few lessons, I was hooked. I continued learning web development and joined bootcamps along the way in my quest to becoming a world-class frontend engineer.

These resources, as great as they were, did not succeed in making me the kind of engineer I envisioned for myself. After reading reviews of AltSchool online, I decided, why not? And here I am.
